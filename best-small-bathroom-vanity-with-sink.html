We assembled each vanity from the box, timing the build and noting where instructions or hardware slowed us down. Floating models got mounted to a stud wall so we could judge how solid they feel once they bear weight, since a wall-hung small bathroom vanity with sink lives or dies on its anchors.

After assembly we ran water through each faucet rough-in, filled and drained the sink, and wiped down the tops to see how they shrug off splashes and toothpaste. We loaded the drawers and cabinets with everyday bathroom gear to confirm the storage earns its space instead of just looking good, and we kept each unit in service for several weeks to watch for swelling, sagging shelves, or loosening hinges.

Frequently Asked Questions

What size vanity is best for a small bathroom?

For most small bathrooms, a 36-inch vanity like our top pick the DELUXE LIVING 36 Inch is the sweet spot. It fits a comfortable sink and some storage while leaving walking room. If your space is very tight, a floating 36-inch model such as the SOLIDEE keeps the floor clear and makes the room feel larger.

Do these small bathroom vanities come with a sink?

Yes. Every vanity in this guide ships with the sink and countertop included, so you don't have to buy a separate basin or top. That keeps the listed price the full cost of the unit.

Is a floating or freestanding vanity better for a small bathroom?

A floating vanity frees the floor underneath, which makes a small bathroom feel more open and easier to clean, but it needs solid wall anchors or studs to mount safely. A freestanding cabinet like our top pick is simpler to install and offers enclosed storage. Choose floating for the airy look and freestanding for easy setup and more concealed storage.

How much should I spend on a small bathroom vanity with sink?

Our tested picks range from $269.90 for the budget SOLIDEE to $1,229.99 for the premium DELUXE LIVING 60 Inch. For a small bathroom, you can get a complete, good-quality vanity in the $270 to $360 range, which is where our top pick and budget pick both land.
