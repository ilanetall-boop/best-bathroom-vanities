Things to Know Before You Buy

  • Measure your rough-in first. A vanity top under $500 only fits well if the cabinet width and drain placement match your existing plumbing, so measure the space before adding anything to your cart. Our sizing guide walks through exactly what to check.
  • Engineered wood dominates this price range. Every vanity in this guide uses MDF or engineered wood construction rather than solid hardwood, which keeps costs down but means you should wipe up standing water quickly.
  • An attached sink top saves you a separate purchase. Most of the picks below include a mounted basin as part of the cabinet, so the listed price covers the full top and sink together, rather than the cabinet alone.
  • Cabinet width should leave clearance on both sides. Leave a couple of inches between the vanity and any wall, tub, or toilet so doors and drawers open fully; our install guide covers spacing in more detail.
  • More drawers usually beat more counter space in a small bathroom. If you are working with a tight footprint, a narrower option from our small bathroom vanities guide may serve you better than a wide top you cannot fully open.

How We Picked

We started with a wide pool of vanities priced under $500 and built this list of the best vanity tops under $500 by first checking whether the listing included a real sink top or a cabinet alone. Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best vanity top under $500?

For most bathrooms, the Yaheetech 30" Bathroom Vanity with is the best vanity top under $500. It pairs a full-size 30-inch cabinet with an attached sink top for about $218, giving you more counter space and storage than the narrower or cheaper options in this guide. If your bathroom can't fit a 30-inch cabinet, the Elysionix 24" Single Bathroom Vanity offers the same setup in a smaller footprint.

Do vanity tops under $500 include the sink?

Most do, but not all. Five of the seven vanities in this guide, including our top pick, ship with the sink basin already mounted to the top, so the listed price covers the full countertop and sink together. The HOMCOM Under Sink Bathroom Cabinet is the exception, since it's a storage cabinet meant to sit under an existing sink rather than a replacement top. Check the listing carefully before you buy if a built-in sink is a must-have.

How long do budget vanity tops last?

The engineered-wood vanities in this price range typically hold up for five to ten years of normal bathroom use if you wipe up standing water and avoid letting puddles sit around the sink cutout. Because these tops use MDF or engineered wood rather than solid stone, they are more sensitive to prolonged moisture than a quartz or granite top, so quick cleanup after daily use goes a long way toward extending their life.
