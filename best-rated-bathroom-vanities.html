Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best rated bathroom vanity in 2026?

For most bathrooms, the LIKIMIO 59-inch vanity at $629.99 is the best rated bathroom vanity, thanks to its roomy single-sink top and soft-close storage. For a small space, the SOLIDEE 36-inch at $269.90 is our pick, and shared bathrooms do well with the 72-inch dual-sink eclife at $999.99.

Are MDF bathroom vanities durable enough for a bathroom?

Yes, with a little care. Every vanity in this guide uses MDF or engineered wood, which holds paint well and keeps the price down. The main weakness is moisture at the cut edges, so seal exposed edges, fix dripping faucets quickly, and wipe up standing water. Owners who do that get years of use out of these cabinets.

What size bathroom vanity should I buy?

Measure your wall width, your plumbing location, and the door swing first. A 36-inch unit like the SOLIDEE suits a powder room, 59 and 60-inch models such as the LIKIMIO and fatani fit most full bathrooms, and a 72-inch dual-sink vanity like the eclife or Mirightone works when two people share the space and the wall is long enough.
