Frequently Asked Questions

Do these bathroom vanity mirrors with lights come with the lights included?

It depends on the pick. The Floating Vanity 36" includes a smart mirror, and the B Beauty Planet is a standalone lighted mirror. The full vanity sets, like the LUXOAK and Vanity Art, sell the cabinet on its own, so you add a lighted mirror separately.

How much should I spend on a vanity mirror with lights?

Our picks run from $27.99 for the B Beauty Planet standalone mirror to $1,614.99 for the XWNE walnut vanity. A simple lighted mirror covers grooming for under $30, while a full vanity with an integrated smart mirror, like our $1,445.68 top pick, bundles storage and lighting together.

Are MDF bathroom vanities durable?

MDF holds up well if you keep water from sitting on it. Most vanities in this roundup use MDF or engineered wood, including the LIKIMIO and DELUXE LIVING. Wipe up splashes promptly and seal any exposed edges, and an MDF vanity will last for years.
